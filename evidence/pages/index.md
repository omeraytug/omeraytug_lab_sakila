This dashboard presents an exploratory data analysis (EDA) of the **Sakila film rental database**
using **DuckDB** and **Evidence**.

The goal is to provide management with insights into:

- Rental activity over time
- Revenue performance
- Film and category popularity
- Customer behavior

The analysis is based on tasks completed in **Task 1 and Task 2**, and extended with additional
exploratory findings.
